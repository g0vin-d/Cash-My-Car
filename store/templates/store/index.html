How it works

1. Login

The first step here is to login. The new user has to register first.

2. Get a free evaluation

Our website will inspect your car and verify the information of your car. Once the inspection is completed, we will provide you with an estimate valuation of your car.

3. Sell your car

Get an offer from our channel partners for your car. Once you accept the offered price don't worry, we'll take care of all the paperwork and RC transfer. Get your payment in an instant transfer to your account.

Using machine learning we are going to develop a website which will evaluate the proper value for your car by taking the inputs from the user like, brand of car, kilometres driven, year of purchase etc. Also here is an option called SELLING PORTAL the sellers are allowed to post the details of their car for sale.
